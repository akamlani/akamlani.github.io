Kamlani - march-madness 2014-04-15T12:09:59-07:00 http://arikamlani.com/blogs/jekyll/tag/march-madness.html Ari Kamlani march madness sports analytics 2014-03-30T00:00:00-07:00 http://arikamlani.com/blogs/jekyll//blogs/jekyll/2014/03/30/march-madness-sports-analytics <h4>march madness sports analytics</h4> <p>Nate Silver has gained a lot of recognition in regards to analyzing MLB stats and political forecasting, and now he has taken his talents to the <a href="http://fivethirtyeight.com/interactives/march-madness-predictions/">NCAA March Madness bracket</a>. However there there are a lot of upsets in the bracket, so the record and stats alone don't tell the entire story. Some methods place a particular emphasis on a certain spect, such as the <a href="http://sonnymoorepowerratings.com/m-basket.htm">Moore ratings</a> on recent performance. There are adjustments made, e.g. injuries, travel distance, tourney updates.</p> <p>Kentucky(8) only had a 4% chance of reaching the Championship, and Connecticut(7) had even less of a chance at 2%, with Florida being the only #1 seed to advance to the Final Four. The selection committee seedings (RPI) didn't to some teams justice in the rankings, such as Kentucky ranked as a #8 seed. While Louisville had a good chance of repeating at 24%, it got knocked out earlier, and 34-0 wichita State got knocked out by Kentucky in an earlier round as well.</p> <p>It's tough to predict the champions, who would have predicted #7 Seed UConn.</p>
