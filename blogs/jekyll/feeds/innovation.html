There is in reference to an article in wired <a href="http://www.wired.com/2013/11/innovation-the-most-important-and-overused-word-in-america/">innovation the most important and overused keyword in america</a>.</p> <p>To be innovative, it should be seen as a process, a repetitive process. We need to break the cycle.</p> <p>Although I do agree the it is an overused word with those trying to stand out from the rest of the pack.
